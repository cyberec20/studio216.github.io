The Metric of Obsession.

Why we are called Studios 216.

216 is not a random number. It is a receipt.

When we set out to build WattsWise, our flagship product, we faced a market full of outdated, clunky tools. We didn't want to just build an app; we wanted to architect a definitive solution for electrical engineers.

We used an AI-assisted engineering workflow — automation, rapid iteration, and careful validation — to compress what often takes months into a focused sprint. But it wasn't magic. It was 216 hours of relentless coding, logic verification, and design iteration to reach version 1.0.

That number reminds us that speed and quality come from strong engineering plus automation — not one-to-one consulting. Studios216 builds apps and SaaS products that users can run themselves.

Our Philosophy

  • We don't ship "good enough". We ship precision.
  • We automate the tedious to focus on the creative.
  • We build tools we actually use.
