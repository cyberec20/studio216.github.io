Why we are called Studios 216.

216 is not a random number. It is a receipt.

When we set out to build WattsWise, our flagship product, we faced a market full of outdated, clunky tools. We didn't want to just build an app; we wanted to architect a definitive solution for electrical engineers.

We leveraged advanced AI architecture and deep domain expertise to compress months of development into a focused sprint. But it wasn't magic. It was 216 hours of relentless coding, logic verification, and design iteration to reach version 1.0.

That number reminds us that quality comes from the seamless integration of human expertise and artificial intelligence.
