User Data Deletion

Last updated: December 21, 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of user data associated with the WattsWise mobile application (developed by Studios216).

1) What data can be deleted

WattsWise does not require account registration. However, the app uses third-party services for analytics and ads measurement, which may collect device and usage information, such as:

  • App usage events and aggregated analytics
  • Crash/diagnostic data (to improve stability)
  • Advertising identifiers and ad performance/measurement signals

Third-party providers used by the app may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

2) How to request deletion

To request deletion of data associated with your use of WattsWise, please email us with the details below.

Email: wattswise@studios216.com

Subject: WattsWise — Data Deletion Request

Please include:

  • The app name: WattsWise
  • Your country/region (optional)
  • Approximate dates when you used the app (optional)
  • If available, your Android Advertising ID (optional)
  • Device model and Android version (optional)

We will review your request and delete the data we control that is reasonably associated with your provided information. For data processed by third-party services (Firebase/AdMob/Google Play services), deletion may require additional time and may be subject to the provider’s policies and technical constraints.

3) Response time

We typically respond within a reasonable timeframe. Complex requests or requests with insufficient identifying information may take longer or require follow-up questions.

4) Payments and subscriptions

If you have a paid subscription or one-time purchase, please note that billing is handled by Google Play. We do not store your payment details. If you need to manage or cancel subscriptions, use your Google Play subscription settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at wattswise@studios216.com.
