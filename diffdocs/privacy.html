Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Your privacy matters. DIF Docs is designed to minimize data retention and maximize transparency.

Information We Collect

If you create an account, we may collect basic information such as your email address and authentication identifiers.

We may collect limited technical data required to operate the Service, such as job identifiers, timestamps, and system logs.

Document Handling

DIF Docs does not permanently store user documents. Uploaded files are processed temporarily, results are generated for download, and files are automatically deleted after processing or retention timeout.

We do not analyze document content for marketing, profiling, or training purposes.

Analytics and Tracking

Analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar) may be enabled to understand usage patterns, feature adoption, and subscription conversions.

Analytics can be enabled, disabled, or configured depending on deployment stage and environment.

Payments

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. DIF Docs does not store full payment card details.

Payment providers handle billing data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be shared only when required by law, for security purposes, or with infrastructure providers strictly necessary to operate the Service.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page.
