Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know about our pricing plans

What is a credit and how is it consumed? +
One credit equals one document comparison (one job). Each time you upload two documents and generate a comparison, exactly 1 credit is consumed.
What's the difference between subscription and pay-as-you-go credits? +
Pay-as-you-go credits ($9.99 for 25 credits): One-time purchase, credits never expire, and accumulate over time. Perfect for occasional users.

Subscription credits ($12.99/month or $119/year): Included with monthly/annual plans, provide much better value per credit, but unused credits do not roll over to the next period. Ideal for regular users.
Which credits are used first? +
Subscription credits are always consumed before pay-as-you-go credits. This ensures you use your monthly allowance first before dipping into your permanent credit balance.
Do my credits expire? +
Pay-as-you-go credits: Never expire. Once purchased, they remain in your account indefinitely.

Subscription credits: Do not roll over. Each month (or year) you receive a fresh allocation of credits. Any unused credits from the previous period are lost.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access to any remaining subscription credits until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal through our secure payment processor.
Do you offer refunds? +
Full refunds are available within 14 days of purchase if zero credits have been consumed. Once you use any credit, refunds are not available. See our Refund Policy for details.
